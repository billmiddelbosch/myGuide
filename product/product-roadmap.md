# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Landing Page
Marketing page explaining cityCast's features and value proposition to convert visitors into users.

### 2. Address Pages
Dynamic, SEO-optimized landing pages for individual addresses showing street descriptions, city info, nearby tour stops, and CTAs to drive organic search traffic into the tour experience.

### 3. Tour Stop Pages
SEO-optimized landing pages for individual cityCast tour stops, designed to rank in search results with audio playback, interactive maps, and CTAs to the tour builder.

### 4. Tour Builder
Create personalized tours by selecting location, available time, and POI types of interest.

### 5. Tour Experience
Audio playback with AI-generated narration, GPS-triggered stops, and route guidance.

### 6. My Tours
Save, download, and manage tours for offline access.
